Recent research has shown that our brains don’t get rid of old ways of thinking very quickly. Old neural pathways or habits remain ready to be reactivated if we don’t give our brains an alternative. However, every time we respond differently to a familiar situation, or try to, we forge new neural pathways on top of the old ones. The more we practice, the stronger the new pathway gets, and the weaker the old one gets. What new pathways are you forging?
